The script 01_spatial_data_from_gee.R gives details on downloading data to put in this folder. The files that should appear in this site is the unzipped folder from the Zenodo archive for Griscom et al. 2017 PNAS available here (https://zenodo.org/record/883444) and the human footprint raster to be labelled HFP2009.tif available here (https://sedac.ciesin.columbia.edu/data/set/wildareas-v3-2009-human-footprint).
